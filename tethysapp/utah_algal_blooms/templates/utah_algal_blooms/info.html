Background

In recent years, water bodies across the state of Utah have experienced an increase in blue-green algae, commonly referred to as harmful algal blooms. These algal blooms contain toxins that can be harmful, even deadly, to most animals, including humans. This app is designed to keep track of harmful algal blooms as they appear in lakes/reservoirs throughout Utah.

How it Works

When an algal bloom occurs, for example in Utah Lake, you can add descriptive information to the table (location, year, severity, etc). You can also use the interactive map to add a point at the location in Utah Lake the bloom occurred which will store the coordinates and allow the point to appear on the map on the home page.
